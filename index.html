Jean Paul Gaultier "Le Parfum"

Experience the bold allure of Jean Paul Gaultier's Le Male, starting with refreshing mint and bergamot. The heart features a warm blend of lavender and cinnamon, leading to a rich base of creamy vanilla and sandalwood. Embrace your individuality and let this captivating scent tell your story.

Jean Paul Gaultier "Le Beau Paradise Garden"

Immerse yourself in the seductive freshness of Le Beau, where vibrant bergamot and juicy coconut create an invigorating opening. The heart reveals a floral elegance with warm notes of tonka bean, embodying a sun-kissed charm. Finally, the fragrance settles into a rich base of wood and vanilla, leaving a lasting impression of masculinity and allure.

Jean Paul Gaultier "Le Male Elixir"

Unveil the intensity of Le Male Elixir, a bold reinterpretation of the classic. It opens with a striking blend of fresh citrus and spicy cardamom, creating an electrifying first impression. The heart reveals rich notes of lavender and anise, while the base deepens with warm woods and a hint of sweet vanilla, resulting in a powerful and seductive fragrance that leaves an unforgettable mark.
